Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 04, 2026

At TechTools, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website.

GDPR Data Protection Rights (European Users)

Under GDPR, users have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of personal data.

Since TechTools does not directly collect personal information, most GDPR provisions may not apply.

Users may contact us for any privacy-related concerns.

CCPA and CPRA Privacy Rights (California Residents)

California residents have the right to know what personal data is collected, used, or shared.

TechTools does not sell or trade personal information.

Any data collected by third-party services is governed by their respective privacy policies.
