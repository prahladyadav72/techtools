A payslip (also called a salary slip) is an official document provided to employees each pay period showing gross earnings, deductions, and net pay. Beyond being a simple receipt, payslips serve as proof of income for loan applications, tax filings, rental agreements, and personal financial records.

Why Use an Online Payslip Generator?

Creating payslips manually in Word or Excel is slow and error-prone. What's Included in a Payslip

A well-structured payslip covers the company header (name and address), employee details (name, ID, department), pay period information, an earnings breakdown (basic salary, HRA, allowances, bonuses), a deductions breakdown (PF, tax, ESI), and the final net pay. This tool lets you customize every field to match your organization's payroll structure.
